Privacy Policy Divine Wisdom: Daily Hindu Scriptures & Spiritual Insights Effective Date: April 10, 2026 1. Introduction This Privacy Policy explains how the Chrome extension "Divine Wisdom: Daily Hindu Scriptures & Spiritual Insights" (the "Extension") handles your information. We are committed to being transparent about our practices. Please read this policy carefully to understand what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used. 2. Who We Are This Extension is an independent Chrome extension that delivers daily verses and spiritual insights from Hindu scriptures such as the Bhagavad Gita, Puranas, and Vedas. We are not affiliated with Google or any religious organization. 3. What Information We Collect We want to be clear: this Extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information about you. The Extension uses the following browser permissions: Storage Permission: We use Chrome's built-in storage feature to save your personal preferences locally on your device. This includes things like your favorite verses, display settings, or notification preferences. This data stays on your device only and is never sent to any server. Tabs and Active Tab Permissions: The Extension uses these permissions to display spiritual content overlays or popups when you open a new tab or interact with the Extension. We do not read, record, monitor, or transmit any information about the websites you visit or your browsing activity. 4. What We Do Not Collect We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information. We do not collect your browsing history or the URLs of pages you visit. We do not collect your location, device identifiers, or IP address. We do not collect financial information or payment data. We do not use cookies or any third-party tracking technologies. We do not share any data with third parties, advertisers, or analytics services. 5. How We Use Local Storage The Extension stores your preferences and saved content directly on your device using Chrome's local storage API. This data is used only to remember your settings between sessions so you have a consistent experience. You can clear this data at any time by removing the Extension from Chrome or clearing your browser's extension storage through Chrome settings. 6. Content Scripts The Extension includes a content script that runs on web pages you visit. This script is used solely to display spiritual content or UI elements as part of the Extension's features. The content script does not read, copy, record, or transmit any content from the websites you visit or any personal information entered on those pages. 7. No Data Sharing We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any data with any third parties. Since we do not collect personal data in the first place, there is nothing to share. 8. Children's Privacy This Extension is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Extension, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps. 9. Security Since all data stays on your local device and no information is transmitted to external servers, the security risks associated with data collection do not apply to this Extension. We rely on Chrome's built-in security for local storage. 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the Extension after any changes means you accept the updated policy. 11. Your Rights Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information for us to provide, correct, or delete on request. If you wish to remove all locally stored data related to this Extension, you can do so by uninstalling the Extension from Chrome or clearing its local data through Chrome's settings under Extensions. 12. Contact Us If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about this Privacy Policy or the Extension, please feel free to reach out to us through the Chrome Web Store listing page for this Extension. We are happy to address any questions you may have. This privacy policy is provided in plain English and is intended to be clear and easy to understand. We are committed to your privacy and to delivering a safe, trustworthy spiritual experience.
